Welcome to Neo Chiropractic
At Neo Chiropractic clinic, we place strong emphasis in restoring the alignment of the spine with state-of-the-art chiropractic adjustment techniques and adjunct therapies. Once the spine is optimally aligned, we are able to eliminate nerve irritations, hence allowing the body to heal itself.
Our aim is to provide each individual a professional, personalized and patient-centric service to help them manage and overcome their conditions, thereby achieving maximum health and function.
We have treated patients ranging from children to the elderly, and also athletes that suffer from sports-related injuries. We commit ourselves fully to helping the community achieve their health and wellbeing goals, and leading them back of the path of wellness through effective chiropractic care.
